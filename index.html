The Monkees

The Monkees is a rock band formed in 1966, whose components are originally from the city of Los Angeles, United States. The group was originally created for a television comedy with the same name on the NBC.

Micky Dolenz, Davy Jones, Peter Tork and Michael Nesmith were selected from more than 500 young people.
